What is Plants4Non-Profit?

Plants4Non-Profit is a non-profit dedicated to selling plants to donate to other non-profits like SecondHarvest. We hope to raise enough money to help make a difference

Who are we?

Our members are students all coming from a neighborhood together. We all decided to make this non-profit together and grow plants together to sell for non-profits.

Where are we located?

We are located in San Jose California and sell in that area. We do not live in any other cities and cannot sell there.

Who do we support?

We support inFUSE, a non-profit dedicated to helping special needs kids. We also support Second Harvest, a non-profit dedicated to helping homeless people have food to eat
